# Final Product Vision

WorkShelf aims to provide a premium online marketplace that combines the book discovery experience of Barnes & Noble, the business purchasing efficiency of Amazon Business, and practical business workflow concepts inspired by Zatiq Easy.

The platform will serve both individual customers and business organizations through a modern, scalable MERN-stack architecture.
